Instructions
There are 8 images and descriptions, we ask that you rate them based on:
  • Grammar. Focusing on description text only, rate the grammar and correctness of the description. Please take into account spelling errors, bad grammar and awkward word usage.
  • Appropriateness. Focusing on both the description and the image, rate appropriate if the details in description are all relevant to the image, and irrelevant otherwise. Here do not focus on the length or amount of details of the description
  • Essential vs Comprehensive. Focusing on both the description and the image, rate Essential for descriptions that only describe the most important details of the image, and Comprehensive for descriptions that contain all relevant details of the image.
